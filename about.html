About Me

From Business Idea to Tech Professional: My Journey in Cloud Computing

Hi, I'm Sade, a highly motivated professional with diverse experience in the public sector, currently transitioning from my successful career as a Free Early Education Entitlement Coordinator and Housing Specialist to pursue my passion for Cloud Engineering and Development. I am enrolled in the rigorous AWS re/Start Cloud Computing Bootcamp, where I am developing core IT fundamental skills in AWS, Cloud Computing, Linux, Bash Scripting, Python Programming, Cloud Security and Databases, and I am expected to achieve the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner Certification upon completion of the program.

As a creative individual with a passion for using technology to bring my ideas to life, I am excited to apply my technical expertise to create innovative solutions that meet the specific needs of organisations and contribute to their growth and success. I love writing both fictional and non-fictional works and creating blog content around AI and how it can be used to enhance creativity. I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for artists, and I'm always looking for new ways to incorporate it into my writing process.

My diverse work history includes experiences as a Housing Assistant, Paralegal Assistant, and Customer Service Representative, which have allowed me to develop skills in negotiation, case management, client services, and customer satisfaction. As a Systems Support Assistant at Bristol City Council, I leverage my proficiency in cloud computing, networking, Python programming, security, and databases to improve data quality and support system development and enhancement requests. I also demonstrated strong communication skills by assisting parents with the application process and guiding childcare providers through the registration process. I streamlined a system to notify parents of their application results, taking the SLA from 14 days to 7 days.

When I'm not working or studying, you can usually find me experimenting with new technologies, exploring the historic city of Bath, or spending time with my family and friends. I want to welcome visitors to my page and convey that I am a cloud professional that is looking for new opportunities as a cloud engineer or developer and also that I love to write about technology and AI. Thanks for stopping by!
