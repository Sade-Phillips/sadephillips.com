Cloudy with a Chance of Code

From Business Idea to Tech Professional: My Journey in Cloud Computing

Hi there, I'm excited to share with you my journey to becoming a tech professional. As a career changer with over 10 years in the workforce under my belt, I'm no stranger to overcoming obstacles and pursuing my passions despite the odds.

It all started with a business idea that I had, but I didn't have the financial support to bring it to life through paid avenues. That's when I decided to take matters into my own hands and start learning about software development and tech in general. As I delved deeper into the world of technology, I discovered a passion for it that I never knew existed.

Currently, I'm in the process of completing the AWS re/Start program in cloud computing. This program has given me a strong foundation and the skills necessary to pursue a career as either a cloud or software developer. It's been an intense journey, but one that has been extremely rewarding.

Through my previous work experience and the re/Start program, I've gained a deep understanding of various programming languages, software development methodologies, and cloud computing technologies. This has given me the confidence to pursue not just a career in cloud computing but also to explore my passion for freelancing and technical writing.

I'm excited to see where my journey in tech takes me, and I'm eager to share my experiences with others who are also embarking on a similar path. I believe that it's important to encourage more diversity in tech, and I hope that by sharing my story, I can inspire others to pursue their passions in technology.

In this blog, I'll be sharing my insights and experiences as I continue to grow and learn in the world of tech. From tips on mastering coding languages to navigating the job market as a black millennial, I'll be sharing it all. I hope that my journey will not only inspire others but also serve as a resource for those looking to pursue a career in tech.

Thank you for taking the time to read my story. I'm excited to share this journey with you all and see where it takes us!
